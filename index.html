Noordzij's Cube

Jialin Lu luxxxlucy.github.io November 14 2025

Visualizing Gerrit Noordzij's Cube, which is perhaps the world's first designspace and root of the construction of parametric design and variable font. It introduces the three fundamental characteristics of letterforms, in the cultural sense that the aesthetic of the letterform is determined by the writing tool of pen.
This is now rather boring, in the next step I will try to recreate this with a learning model. Too many works has been done on AI/ML for typography, but I guess in order for it to be truly recognized, we need to be back to classics and be all on focusing on the nuances.
